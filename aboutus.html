Our Profile

We are a team of 8 students from 2 different scientific backgrounds, namely business management and shipping engineering, who are currently undergoing a practical work program at PT PAL Indonesia. We are placed in the Marketing Division, Harkan Sales Department, and assigned to develop a modern computerized system that aims to facilitate the management of ship berthing spaces.

Vision and Mission

Vision

Becoming a department that excels in managing ship docking space with a modern computerized system that supports efficiency, effectiveness, and operational sustainability at PT PAL Indonesia.

Our History

The management of the ship docking room in the Sales and Marketing Division, Harkan Sales Department is still done manually, causing various obstacles in its operation. To improve the efficiency and effectiveness of the management, we were asked to develop a modern computerized system that can help this department in managing the ship docking more optimally.
